Space exploration involves the study of celestial bodies and the exploration of outer space. It is conducted through
both robotic spacecraft and human spaceflights, with the goal of expanding our knowledge of the cosmos and potentially
colonizing other planets. This exploration has led to advancements in various fields, including astronautical
engineering, medicine, and robotics, and has raised questions about the existence of life beyond Earth.
